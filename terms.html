Section 03

Return & Refund Policy

As a food service business, our return and refund policy is designed to be fair while acknowledging the nature of perishable food products.

Refund Eligibility

  • If your order was incorrect due to our error, we will replace it or issue a full refund.
  • If you received a food item that does not meet our quality standards, we will replace or refund.
  • Refund requests must be made at the time of receiving the order or within 30 minutes.

Non-Refundable Situations

  • Change of mind after the order has been prepared.
  • Orders that have been partially or fully consumed.
  • Incorrect orders due to errors in customer-provided information.
  • Spice level or taste preference complaints after ordering.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us immediately at the point of service or via email. Please provide your order number (shown on your receipt) and a brief description of the issue.

Approved refunds for card payments will be processed within 5–7 working days back to the original payment method, depending on your bank's processing time.

Takeaway Orders

For takeaway orders, please check your order before leaving the premises. We may not be able to process refund or replacement requests once you have left our location.
